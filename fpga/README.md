advise emulating this design on an FPGA and giving it a spin before tapeout. 
Currently both you and me are assuming we understand how uart is expected to 
behave. 
For something as simple as UART the gap between our expected behavior and 
reality are likely not going to be that wide, but this gap will only widden 
as you take on more complex protocols. 
So although this isn't required YET, as you progress this will become 
more important to increase your chances of a sucesfull A0.
